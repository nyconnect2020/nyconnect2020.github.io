Broadband Pricing Data

This dataset contains pricing information for both land and mobile internet service offered per company at the borough level.

Form 477

This dataset tracks the internet service providers (ISPs) that are found within each Census block, the type of infrastructure (e.g. fiber, cable) used to deliver internet service, and the speeds of their service.

US Census | American Community Survey (ACS)

We used the ACS for demographic information such as race, income, and age, and for broadband internet subscription rates.

NYC Open Data

From NYC's open data portal we fetched the Neighborhood Tabulation Areas (NTAs) shapefiles, Census block shapefiles, and locations of telecommunications infrastructure.
We also referenced NYC's Internet Master Plan for guidance.
